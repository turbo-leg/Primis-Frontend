# Primis Platform - Website Tour Video Script

**Video Title:** Website Tour: A Complete Walkthrough
**Target Audience:** New users (Students, Parents, Teachers)
**Duration:** Approx. 2-3 minutes

---

## Scene 1: Introduction
**Visual:**
- Screen recording of the Homepage (Hero section).
- Mouse hovering over the logo and navigation bar.

**Audio (Voiceover):**
"Welcome to the Primis College Prep platform. This video will give you a quick tour of our website to help you navigate our features and get the most out of your educational journey."

---

## Scene 2: Homepage & Navigation
**Visual:**
- Scroll down the homepage slowly, showing the "About", "Services", and "Programs" sections.
- Highlight the top navigation bar.

**Audio:**
"Our homepage is your gateway to everything Primis. Here you can find information about our college prep programs, language courses, and our team of expert teachers. Use the top navigation bar to easily access different sections of the site."

---

## Scene 3: Registration & Login
**Visual:**
- Click on the "Register" button.
- Show the registration form.
- Briefly show the "Login" page.

**Audio:**
"To get started, click 'Register' in the top right corner. You can create an account as a Student, Teacher, or Parent. If you already have an account, simply click 'Login' to access your personal dashboard."

---

## Scene 4: The Dashboard
**Visual:**
- Log in as a Student (demo account).
- Show the Student Dashboard overview (Attendance, Grades, Schedule).

**Audio:**
"Once logged in, you'll be taken to your Dashboard. This is your command center.
*   **Students:** You can track your attendance, view upcoming assignments, and check your grades.
*   **Parents:** You can monitor your child's progress and stay in touch with teachers.
*   **Teachers:** You can manage classes, mark attendance, and grade assignments efficiently."

---

## Scene 5: Courses & Enrollment
**Visual:**
- Click on the "Courses" link in the navigation.
- Show the list of available courses.
- Click on a course to show details.

**Audio:**
"Navigate to the 'Courses' page to browse our available programs. You can filter by category, view detailed course descriptions, and enroll directly through the platform."

---

## Scene 6: Tutorials & Support
**Visual:**
- Click on the new "Tutorials" link in the navigation.
- Scroll through the video categories.
- Show the "Contact" page briefly.

**Audio:**
"Need more help? Visit our new 'Tutorials' page for step-by-step video guides on specific features like making payments or submitting assignments. And if you have any questions, our support team is just a click away on the 'Contact' page."

---

## Scene 7: Conclusion
**Visual:**
- Back to Homepage Hero section.
- Fade to Primis Logo with website URL.

**Audio:**
"Thank you for choosing Primis. We are dedicated to empowering your academic excellence. Sign up today and start your journey!"
